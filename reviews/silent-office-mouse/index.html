FAQ

Are silent mice worse for gaming?

Silent mice typically have slightly higher click latency (1-3ms more) due to dampening mechanisms, which is negligible for office work but may be noticeable in competitive gaming.

How much quieter are silent mice?

Silent mice reduce click noise by 80-90%, from ~40-45dB in standard mice down to 28-32dB, comparable to soft keyboard typing.
