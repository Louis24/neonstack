Buying Guide FAQ

How many lumens do I need for outdoor projection?

For complete darkness (backyard movie nights), 200+ ANSI lumens is sufficient. For dusk or ambient light, aim for 400+ ANSI lumens.

Can portable projectors replace a TV?

For occasional viewing, yes. However, portable projectors typically lack the color accuracy and HDR performance of dedicated home theater projectors or OLED TVs.
