Frequently Asked Questions

What is the correct height for a standing desk?

When standing, adjust the desk height so your elbows are bent at a 90-degree angle, with your forearms resting flat on the desktop. The top of your computer monitor should align at or slightly below your eye level.

How long should you stand at a standing desk daily?

Ergonomic experts recommend a sit-stand ratio of 1:1 or 2:1. For every hour of sitting, stand for 30 to 45 minutes. Alternating frequently is far better than standing or sitting for 8 hours straight.
