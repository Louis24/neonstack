FAQ

What's the difference between RGB and RGBIC?

RGB: entire strip shows one color. RGBIC (addressable): each LED displays different colors simultaneously, enabling flowing effects and animations. RGBIC costs 2-3x more.

How bright should LED strips be?

300+ lumens/meter for accent lighting. 600+ lumens/meter for task lighting (desk, kitchen). Check lumens, not just LED count - 5050 SMD LEDs are brighter than 3528.
