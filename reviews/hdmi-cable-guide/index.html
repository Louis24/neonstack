FAQ

Do I need HDMI 2.1?

Only if gaming at 4K 120Hz on PS5/Xbox Series X, or planning 8K content. For standard 4K 60Hz, HDMI 2.0 works fine.

Are expensive HDMI cables better?

No. Digital signal either works or doesn't. Certified cables under $15 perform identically to $50+ cables. Look for "Ultra High Speed HDMI" certification.
