Common Questions

Is mirrorless better than DSLR for beginners?

Yes for most use cases. Mirrorless cameras are lighter, have faster autofocus, and offer real-time exposure preview through the electronic viewfinder.

What lens should I buy first?

Start with the kit lens (usually 16-50mm or 18-55mm). After 3-6 months, assess what focal length you use most and invest in a prime lens like a 35mm f/1.8 or 50mm f/1.8.
