Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best cable management method for standing desks?

Use cable sleeves (not rigid raceways) to bundle cables with enough slack for height adjustment. Mount power strips under the desk using velcro straps so they move with the desk.

How do I remove adhesive cable clips without damage?

Heat the adhesive with a hair dryer for 30 seconds to soften the glue. Slowly peel from one corner. Remove residue with isopropyl alcohol or Goo Gone cleaner.

Can cable management boxes overheat power strips?

Choose ventilated cable boxes with airflow holes. Never exceed the power strip's rated amperage. If the box feels hot to touch, it's overloaded - remove high-wattage devices.

What's the cleanest under-desk cable routing method?

Install a cable raceway along the back edge of your desk, then use cable clips every 6-8 inches to route individual cables. Add a cable box on the floor for the power strip and excess slack.
