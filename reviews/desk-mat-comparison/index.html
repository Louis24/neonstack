FAQ

What material is best?

Cloth mats provide comfort and smooth mouse tracking. Hard plastic offers faster glide for gaming. Leather looks premium but wears faster.

How do I clean a desk mat?

Cloth: hand wash with mild soap, air dry. Hard surface: wipe with damp cloth. Avoid machine washing cloth mats as it damages the rubber backing.
