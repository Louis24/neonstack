Frequently Asked Questions

What does LDAC codec mean for audio quality?

LDAC transmits 3x more data than standard Bluetooth codecs (990kbps vs 328kbps), preserving Hi-Res audio detail. Sony WF-1000XM5 supports LDAC, making them ideal for lossless music streaming from TIDAL or Apple Music.

Which earbuds have the best ANC for flights?

Bose QC Earbuds II achieve -42dB noise cancellation, the deepest reduction available. They excel at blocking low-frequency engine rumble during flights. Sony WF-1000XM5 (-40dB) comes close second.

Are AirPods Pro 2 worth it for Android users?

Not recommended. Android users lose spatial audio, seamless device switching, Find My tracking, and battery optimization features. Choose Sony WF-1000XM5 or Samsung Galaxy Buds2 Pro instead for full Android compatibility.

How important is battery life for daily use?

6+ hours per charge covers most workdays without mid-day charging. Look for 24+ hour total battery (with case) if you travel frequently. Sony WF-1000XM5 leads with 8h + 24h, while AirPods Pro 2 offers 6h + 30h.
