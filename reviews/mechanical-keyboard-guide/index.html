Frequently Asked Questions

Can you use a quiet mechanical keyboard for competitive gaming?

Yes. Quiet switches, such as Gateron Silent Browns or Cherry MX Silent Reds, retain the identical high polling rates and rapid actuation of standard gaming switches, meaning you lose zero competitive performance.

How do you make a mechanical keyboard even quieter?

Beyond selecting silent switches, installing O-rings on keycaps, using a desk mat (desk pad), and choosing a keyboard with built-in case dampening foam will significantly mute the sound profile.
