Frequently Asked Questions

Which smartwatch has the best battery life?

Huawei Watch GT 4 leads with 14 days of typical use. Garmin Forerunner 265 and Xiaomi Watch S2 follow with 13 and 12 days respectively. Apple Watch Series 9 requires daily charging at 18 hours.

Can Android users use Apple Watch?

No. Apple Watch requires an iPhone to function. Android users should consider Huawei, Xiaomi, Samsung Galaxy Watch, or Garmin models instead.

Which watch is best for marathon training?

Garmin Forerunner 265 provides the most comprehensive training metrics including VO2 max estimation, training load balance, race predictor, and recovery advisor - features that serious runners rely on.

What's the best value smartwatch?

Xiaomi Watch S2 at under $200 offers the best price-to-feature ratio. You get 12-day battery, AMOLED display, comprehensive health tracking, and 117 sports modes - features that cost 2-3x more from premium brands.
