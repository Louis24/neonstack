FAQ

Do smart plugs need a hub?

Most modern smart plugs connect directly to WiFi without a hub. Check compatibility with your router's 2.4GHz network (5GHz not supported by most plugs).

Can smart plugs save energy?

Smart plugs with energy monitoring show real-time power usage. Schedule devices to turn off automatically when not needed. Typical savings: 5-10% on standby power consumption.
