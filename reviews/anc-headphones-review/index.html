Frequently Asked Questions

Do ANC headphones protect your hearing from loud noise?

Yes. By electronically counteracting background sounds, they allow you to listen to music or study in quiet environments at much lower volume levels, effectively preventing noise-induced hearing loss.

Can Active Noise Cancellation block out human voices?

ANC is naturally most effective against low-frequency, steady hums (like airplane cabins). However, top-tier models like the Sony XM5 use advanced AI filters to significantly deaden human chatter and speech frequencies as well.
