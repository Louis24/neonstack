FAQ

What is CADR rating?

Clean Air Delivery Rate measures cubic feet per minute (CFM) of filtered air. For desk purifiers, 50+ CFM is adequate. Larger offices need 100+ CFM.

How often replace HEPA filters?

Every 6-12 months depending on usage. Check filter indicator lights. Pre-filters should be vacuumed monthly to extend HEPA filter life.
