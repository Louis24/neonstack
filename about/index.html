About

Practical pages for small, repeatable decisions.

NeonStack publishes lightweight browser tools, comparison guides, and quick game helpers. The goal is simple: open the page, get the answer, move on.

Most tools run locally in the browser, which keeps them fast and private. Review pages are written to help readers compare tradeoffs before buying or choosing a product category.
